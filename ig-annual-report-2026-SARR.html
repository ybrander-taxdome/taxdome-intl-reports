Reconciliation — SARR vs HubSpot
Why SARR and HubSpot don't tie. SARR (billed) grew +$244,066 Jan–May. HubSpot recorded new bookings $210,978, logged expansion $35,653, logged churn $103,356. The two systems measure different things: HubSpot is a CRM bookings/activity ledger (multi-year, ramp, demo-date timing), and it actually over-states churn ($103,356 logged vs $46,706 true SARR churn — it logs downgrades/non-renewals that never zeroed out Stripe billing). Use SARR for ARR, NRR and GRR; use HubSpot only for GTM activity (pipeline, bookings volume).
